Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about decrypting VR files.

What is a .wsdcf file?

A .wsdcf file is an encrypted video format used by DMM/Fanza. YumeLayer decrypts this format into standard MP4 so you can play it anywhere.

Can I play decrypted files on Quest or Pico?

Yes! Once YumeLayer converts the file to MP4, you can transfer it to your Meta Quest 3, Quest 2, or Pico headset and play it using Skybox, Hereshpere, Moon or other player.

Is YumeLayer free?

YumeLayer has a free trial available on Itch.io.

Will converting my assets affect their quality?

No. YumeLayer ensures your content retains its original quality (8K → 8K, 4K → 4K) while making it compatible with your chosen player.

Is YumeLayer safe to use?

Absolutely. Your assets are processed locally and securely. We respect your privacy and do not store or distribute your content. Internet is only used to validate your KEY. You don’t need to provide your account password.

Do I need technical skills to use YumeLayer?

Not at all. YumeLayer is designed for simplicity. Within a few steps, you can convert and manage your digital assets effortlessly.

Can I use YumeLayer on other platforms?

No. YumeLayer is currently available only on Windows. Please refer to the System Requirements section for more details.

Can I share assets processed with YumeLayer?

No. All assets are strictly for personal use. Any distribution that infringes copyright will lead to immediate revocation of your access and a ban from using the tool.
